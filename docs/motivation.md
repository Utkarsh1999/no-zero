# Motivation Engine: NoZero

## 1. Identity-Based Reinforcement
NoZero shifts the focus from "doing an action" to "becoming a person." The messaging system reflects this shift.

### Language Patterns
- **Instead of**: "Good job tracking today."
- **Use**: "You are proving your identity as a disciplined person."

## 2. Reinforcement Styles
Users can customize the tone of the application's feedback:
- **Soft**: Encouraging and empathetic.
- **Neutral**: Stoic and factual.
- **Aggressive**: Intense and challenging.

## 3. Milestones & Titles
| Level | Requirement | Title |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | 3-Day Streak | The Spark |
| 2 | 14-Day Streak | The Reliable |
| 3 | 30-Day Streak | The Architect |
| 4 | 90% Consistency | The Unstoppable |

## 4. Recovery Messaging
When a miss or relapse occurs, the engine provides "Relapse-Safe" messaging to prevent shame and encourage immediate return to the habit.
- **Comeback Line**: "The comeback is where real resilience is built. Welcome back."
